“tens of thousands of tons of missiles”

If true, that belays concern that Russia has already started to reposition ammunition and that there are no more stockpiles left to hit, so there should be no need to use long-range Western weapons since there are no more ultra-valuable military targets. Seems incorrect! There were also reports of a third ammo depot being hit - the 23rd arsenal in the Tver region. So, I think Russia is failing to learn its lesson time and time again. We’ll see how this plays out on the battlefield, and if Russia becomes noticeably degraded.
